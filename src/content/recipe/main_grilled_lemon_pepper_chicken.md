### Ingredients
* 1 Kg Chicken Tenders
* 1 cup olive oil
* 2/3 cup lemon juice
* 2 tsp minced garlic
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* 1/2 tsp salt

### Instructions
1. Whisk Ingredients together
1. Add chicken to marinade
1. Allow to marinate for 8 hours
1. Grill chicken on a hot pan and add additional pepper as required.
